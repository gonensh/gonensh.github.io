  Welcome to the Russell Conwell Center (RCC). The RCC is comprehensive academic and learning center that enables students to maximize their university experience, achieve academic success, enhance or develop strong leadership skills and prepare for their future careers. The RCC is committed to help support enter and excel in college, attain their undergraduate degree, and enroll in graduate school (if that is your goal).  
 
Mission:

The Russell Conwell Center will provide students with comprehensive support to facilitate academic excellence, as they also progress toward the fulfillment of their academic, career, and life goals. In honoring the learning process the Center will foster a community of scholars, which recognizes the intrinsic value, diversity, and integrity of each individual.
